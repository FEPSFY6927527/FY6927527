Abstract

The Formula One Halo was introduced in 2018, It was brought in under the FIA as a compulsory addition to the car meant to protect the drivers after a series of accidents which either killed or severely injured multiple drivers In this study I’ve looked into the development of the halo, used resources and references to make an analysis and display the statistics of the halo, Its earlier developmental stages and its variations and how its saved multiple drivers since its introduction. It’ll also be looking at various CAD and FEA simulations and breaking down their results to show how the halo was chosen out of its variations by using the FEA simulations as direct comparisons via airflow management and visibility for the driver, It’ll also breakdown the reason why the halo was implemented due to the number of injuries which could of been prevented by the halo showing how critical it was to be implemented.
